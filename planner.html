Frequently Asked Questions

Our vacation planning service includes creating a personalized itinerary based on your preferences, booking accommodations, making restaurant reservations, arranging activities and experiences, and providing you with a comprehensive vacation document. We handle all the bookings for you, and you pay one total amount that includes our service fee.

Our service fee is calculated as a percentage of the total vacation cost, typically ranging from 10-15% depending on the complexity of the itinerary and the services included. The exact fee will be clearly indicated in your vacation plan before you confirm the booking.

After you submit the form, we'll create a personalized vacation plan based on your preferences within 48 hours. We'll send this plan to you for review, and you can request any adjustments. Once you approve the plan, we'll handle all the bookings and send you a confirmation with a comprehensive vacation document.

Yes, you can request changes to your vacation plan after booking, subject to availability and any change fees imposed by the service providers. We'll do our best to accommodate your requests, but please note that changes made close to your arrival date may be limited.

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the services included in your vacation plan. Generally, cancellations made 30+ days before arrival receive a full refund minus the service fee. Cancellations made 15-29 days before arrival receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 15 days before arrival are non-refundable. The specific cancellation policy for your booking will be clearly stated in your vacation plan.
