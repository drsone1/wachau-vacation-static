2. Booking and Payment Terms

2.1 Booking Process

The booking process consists of the following steps:

  1. Client submits vacation preferences through our planning form
  2. We create a personalized vacation plan within 48 hours
  3. Client reviews the plan and requests any adjustments
  4. Client approves the final plan
  5. Client makes payment
  6. We confirm all bookings and send comprehensive vacation documentation

2.2 Payment Terms

Our payment terms are as follows:

  • A 25% deposit is required to confirm your booking
  • The remaining balance is due 30 days before your arrival date
  • For bookings made less than 30 days before arrival, full payment is required at the time of booking

2.3 Service Fee

Our service fee is included in the total price and is calculated as a percentage of the total vacation cost, typically ranging from 10-15% depending on the complexity of the itinerary and the services included. The exact fee will be clearly indicated in your vacation plan before you confirm the booking.

The service fee covers our planning services, booking management, documentation preparation, and customer support throughout your vacation.

3. Cancellation and Refund Policy

3.1 Cancellation by Client

Our standard cancellation policy is as follows:

  • Cancellations made 60+ days before arrival: Full refund minus the service fee
  • Cancellations made 30-59 days before arrival: 75% refund minus the service fee
  • Cancellations made 15-29 days before arrival: 50% refund minus the service fee
  • Cancellations made less than 15 days before arrival: No refund

Please note that specific accommodations, activities, or services may have their own cancellation policies that differ from our standard policy. These will be clearly communicated in your vacation plan.

3.2 Modifications to Bookings

Modifications to your booking may be possible subject to availability and any change fees imposed by service providers. Requests for modifications should be made in writing as early as possible. We cannot guarantee that all modification requests can be accommodated, especially those made close to your arrival date.

3.3 Cancellation by Service Providers

In the rare event that a service provider (hotel, restaurant, activity provider, etc.) cancels your booking, we will make every reasonable effort to find a suitable alternative of equal or higher quality at no additional cost to you.
